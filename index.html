Tikal Guatemala

Top Places to Visit Tikal Guatemala

Temple I (Great Jaguar)

This iconic temple stands at 47 meters, built to honor Jasaw Chan K'awiil I.

Great Plaza

The heart of Tikal, surrounded by iconic temples and historical wonders.

Temple IV

The tallest temple at 70 meters, offering breathtaking jungle views.

Lost World Complex

A Maya astronomical complex used for rituals and stargazing.

Central Acropolis

This complex of interconnected buildings was likely a royal residence. The area offers a good example of the urban layout of ancient Tikal

North Acropolis

One of the oldest parts of the city, this area contains numerous tombs and temples, many of which have been excavated.

Mundo Perdido

A cluster of temples located in a more remote part of the site. It's less crowded and provides an immersive experience of Tikal's ancient grandeur.

Tikal Museum

Situated near the entrance of the park, this museum offers insight into the history and discoveries of the Tikal ruins, with displays of artifacts found during excavations
